Lasagna

This simple lasagna is hearty, cheesy, and perfect for a family meal!

Ingredients :

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 24 oz (700g) marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil

Instructions :

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook Noodles: Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare Meat Sauce:In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.Drain excess fat, then add marinara sauce. Simmer for 5-10 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil.
  4. Mix Ricotta Filling:In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, 1 cup of mozzarella, and 1/4 cup of Parmesan. Mix well.
  5. Assemble Lasagna: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place 3 noodles over the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by 1/3 of the meat sauce. Repeat layers (noodles, ricotta, meat sauce) two more times. Top with the remaining 1 cup of mozzarella and 1/4 cup of Parmesan.
  6. Bake: Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
